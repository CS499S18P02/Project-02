Effort and size estimation

  Several requirements are functionality improvements and features that will take some time to complete, but are rather straightforward. The Bulk of the time spent on this project will be spent on the back end to make this website usable for the customer. The customer understands that we are not full time employees and they relize the limitations of what can be done in one semester. However, our team is striving to make a website that will work far better than it has before and to improve the overall quality and usability of the website. With this said, the effort required for this project will be managable.

Size Estimation

  Our group believes the estimated size for this project will be around 3500-4000 lines of code. Our criteria for this estimation is including the number of lines of code we will be writing to include additional features as well as expanding old ones. This also includes the number of lines we will have to clean up, or fix from what we are given at the beginning of this project, as many of them are messy and inconsistent.
