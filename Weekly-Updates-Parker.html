Week 7: 3/19 - 3/25

I spent this week setting up all of the user permissions. The customer sent us a response to one of our messages describing in detail what all of the different user levels were and what permissions each user level had. So I went ahead and made all of the backend and frontend adjustments necessairy to fulfill all of the customer's requirements. The 4 main users are now Restricted Users, Users, Super Users, and Admins. Restricted users are users who can access the site with the loaded in bugs but cannot turn on/off those bugs. They can also view what group they are in but are unable to see who else is in that group. Users are almost the same as Restricted users except they can actually view who else is in their group. Super Users have all of the previous features of a User but are also able to assign people to groups or create new groups. Lastly, Admins have all the previous features of a Super User with the additional ability to turn on/off particular bugs for certain users.
