Conclusions

Throughout this semester we have learned many different things. Here is a highlight list of some of the things we learned:

  • Team Communication: Working in a group of four was something none of us had done before. We had to work around everyones busy senior schedules which meant we had to do a lot of virtual meetings after business hours. Since we did not have an "office" environment we could not see each other working during the day or ask questions and get immediate feedback. We utilized slack, discord, and atom to get our work done as a group. These platforms were key in successfully getting this project done.

  • Website Development: Two of our team members had significant experience developing different websites, but the others did not. Coming from a wide range of backgrounds meant we really had to depend on eachother to get the project done.

  • Customer Management: This was the first time that we were all directly responsible for working with a client to deliver a project. As a group we had to manage communicating directly with the client on a regular basis and also make sure we understood what they wanted us to produce. We had to decide as a group what was feasible to accomplish this semester and let our client know what all we would be able to get done for them.

  • Time Management: This was one of our biggest concerns at the start of the project. Our client gave us a hefty list of requests and we only had a few short months to develop their website. Prioritizing our task list was the most helpful way of working through this challenge. We knew what needed to get done first and could each tackle assignments that were most valuable to the client.

Luckily we did not have to make too many changes from our initial plan that we discussed with SQS. They were very understanding and easy to work with and they really knew what was feasible to accomplish in such a short period of time. If we were to start this project over their wouldn't be many changes we would want to make. We stayed on schedule and got everything done that they wanted. We could have tried to find better meeting times and worked together more in person, but that might not have ever been feasible due to all of our busy schedules.
